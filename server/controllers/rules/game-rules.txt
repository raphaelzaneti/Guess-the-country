Goal: You must "guess the country" from the hints. There are several types of hints, but some of them are not available for some countries :( 

The less hints you use, more points you earn!

Hints: You start the turn with just one hint. To use one more hint, you just have do press the "New hint" button.

9 Hint -6 points
